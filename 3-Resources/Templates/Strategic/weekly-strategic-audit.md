## Reference: Strategic Audit Quick Checks

### Strategy Alignment Red Flags
- >50% of time on reactive work
- No progress on any strategic priority
- Multiple one-way decisions made hastily
- Energy/stocks declining multiple weeks in a row

### Signs of Good Strategy Execution
- A-priorities completed before B-priorities
- Time matches strategic value (important > urgent)
- Options created > options foreclosed
- Work compounds toward long-term vision
