## Quick Reference: Two-Way Door Rules

1. **Decide quickly** - Overthinking reversible decisions is procrastination
2. **Default to action** - When uncertain, bias toward trying
3. **Set a review trigger** - Know when you'll check if this was right
4. **Accept imperfection** - Good enough decisions made fast beat perfect decisions made slow
5. **Learn from outcome** - Use the result to inform future decisions
